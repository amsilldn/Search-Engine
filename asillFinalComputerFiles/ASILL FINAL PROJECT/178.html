Search tips and examples

You can search for experts’ names, areas of expertise, school or department, and keywords in their biography or additional information (if available). Here are some examples of how to narrow your search:

policy analyst
Finds experts whose pages contain either policy OR analyst
“policy analyst
Find experts whose pages contain both policy AND analyst
poli
Finds experts whose pages contain words beginning with poli, such as policy, politics and political
