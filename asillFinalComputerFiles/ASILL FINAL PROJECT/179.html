Stay in touch with the things you love about IU

Keep up with all the stories of how IU influences the state, nation, and world by subscribing to one or all of our e-newsletters. There is no cost and no advertising. Subscribers’ email addresses will remain confidential with IU.

About the publications

  • Inside IU

    Inside IU is Indiana University’s bi-monthly, all-campus e-newsletter, providing current, relevant information to faculty and staff. This multimedia roundup features stories and information on training and grant opportunities available, the quality and diversity of the IU workplace, IU faculty, student and staff excellence and the positive effects the IU campuses have in their communities and throughout the world.

  • Inside IU special edition: IU Board of Trustees

    Inside IU special edition: IU Board of Trustees is a regular, all-campus e-newsletter, providing current, relevant information to faculty, staff and members of the media about action taken at meetings of the IU Board of Trustees.

  • Inside IU Bloomington

    Inside IU Bloomington is the university’s weekly e-newsletter, providing current, relevant information to faculty and staff on the Bloomington campus. This multimedia roundup features stories and information on training and grant opportunities available, the quality and diversity of the IU workplace, IU faculty, student and staff, excellence and the positive effects the Bloomington campus has in its community and throughout the world.

  • Inside IUPUI

    Inside IUPUI is the university’s weekly e-newsletter, providing current, relevant information to faculty and staff on the Indianapolis campus. This multimedia roundup features stories and information on training and grant opportunities available, the quality and diversity of the IU workplace, IU faculty, student and staff excellence and the positive effects the IUPUI campus has in its community and throughout the world.

  • IU in the News

    IU in the News is a daily review of the important news stories relating to Indiana University.

  • JagNews

    JagNews is the university’s weekly e-newsletter, providing current, relevant information to students on the IUPUI campus.
