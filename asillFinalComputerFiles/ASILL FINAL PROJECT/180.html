RSS Feeds

Add IU news feeds to your website

You can add the complete IU news feed to your website using the following URL: https://news.iu.edu/rss

Or, create a custom IU news feed using story tags. Story tags allow you to easily filter IU news by campus, school, department, or topic. You may use as many or as few tags as you wish.

View all story tags

How to create a custom IU news feed

Filter by campus

Options

  • c: stories that are marked for one or more campuses (i.e., campuses on story are “iub” AND “iupui”)
  • cor: stories that are marked for either campus (i.e., campus on story is “iub” OR “iupui”)

Variables

  • iub
  • iupui
  • iue
  • iuk
  • iun
  • ius
  • iusb
  • ipfw

Examples

Stories marked for IUB campus

https://news.iu.edu/rss?c=iub

Stories marked for IUB AND IUPUI campus

https://news.iu.edu/rss?c=iub+iupui

Stories marked for IUB OR IUPUI campus

https://news.iu.edu/rss?cor=iub+iupui

Filter by topic

Options

  • topic: stories that are marked for one or more topics (i.e., topics on story are “arts-and-humanities” AND “campus-life”)
  • topicOr: stories that are marked for any of the topics (i.e., topic on story is “arts-and-humanities” OR “campus-life”)

Variables

  • administration
  • arts-and-humanities
  • business-and-innovation
  • campus-life
  • global
  • health-and-wellness
  • law-and-policy
  • science-and-technology

Examples

Stories marked for “Arts & Humanities”

https://news.iu.edu/rss?topic=arts-and-humanities

Stories marked for “Arts & Humanities” AND “Campus Life”

https://news.iu.edu/rss?topic=arts-and-humanities+campus-life

Stories marked for “Arts & Humanities” OR “Campus Life”

https://news.iu.edu/rss?topicOr=arts-and-humanities+campus-life

Filter by news for

Options

  • nf: stories that are marked for one or more news for (i.e., news for on story are “students” AND “faculty-and-staff”)
  • nfor: stories that are marked for any of the news for (i.e. news for on story is “students” OR “faculty-and-staff”)

Variables

  • faculty-and-staff
  • iu-community
  • media
  • students

Examples

Get all stories marked for “Faculty & Staff”

https://news.iu.edu/rss?nf=faculty-and-staff

Stories marked for “Faculty & Staff” AND “Students”

https://news.iu.edu/rss?nf=faculty-and-staff+students

Stories marked for “Faculty & Staff” OR “Students”

https://news.iu.edu/rss?nfor=faculty-and-staff+students

Filter by tags

Options

t: get stories that match all tags (i.e., tags on story are “college-of-art-and-sciences” AND “michael-a-mcrobbie”)

tor: get stories that match at least one of the tags (i.e., tag on story is “college-of-art-and-sciences” OR “michael-a-mcrobbie”)

Variables

All possible story tags are listed here.

Examples

Get all stories tagged “alumni”:

https://news.iu.edu/rss?t=alumni

Stories tagged “alumni” and “campus center”:

https://news.iu.edu/rss?t=alumni+campus-center

Stories tagged “alumni” or “campus center:

https://news.iu.edu/rss?tor=alumni+campus-center

Stories tagged “alumni” that are also tagged “campus-center” or “awards”: 

https://news.iu.edu/rss?t=alumni&tor=campus-center+awards

Other custom feed options

Variables can also be combined to produce various feeds.

Examples

Stories from IU Bloomington tagged “alumni”:

https://news.iu.edu/rss?c=iub&t=alumni

Stories in topic “Campus Center” that are tagged “alumni”:

https://news.iu.edu/rss?topic=campus-center&t=alumni

Stories tagged “alumni” that are also tagged “campus-center” or “awards”: https://news.iu.edu/rss?t=alumni&tor=campus-center+awards
