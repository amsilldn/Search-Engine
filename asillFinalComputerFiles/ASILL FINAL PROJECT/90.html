Admissions

The University Graduate School at IU Bloomington offers more than 190 doctoral, master’s, and non-degree graduate programs, as well as world-renowned faculty and a diverse student population.

Let us help you find the right program for you. Whether your goal in attending graduate school is to enter a career in academic research, or to take what you’ve learned to gain greater traction in the job market, the University Graduate School at IU Bloomington has the program that will take you where you want to go next.

Search Degrees and Programs now.  Filter your search by Master's Degree, Doctoral and Professional Degree, or Certificates: Graduate.
