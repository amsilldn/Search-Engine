Career Resources

We have what it takes to succeed

When it comes to networking, our global reach is more than 690,000 IU alumni strong. That means we have access to a large and diverse array of expertise, advice, and connections.

This expertise is reflected in the success of our alumni and in the number of professional development events we host to share career best practices, as well as new ideas, skills, and knowledge with fellow alumni.

IU’s contribution to our success doesn’t end at graduation. It continues to provide the support and resources we need to succeed—throughout our careers and our lives!

Career coaching and success go hand in hand

Success is not a final destination—it is a continuous journey. Even the most successful alums strive to improve their knowledge and skills throughout their careers. Wherever we are on our journey to success, help is available from the expert career coaches at the IU Alumni Association.

Our talented and skilled professional coaches can advise you on the best approach at every stage of your career journey—whether it’s making a minor tweak or taking a whole new path.

Learn more about career coaching

And networking online is crucial

Put our massive network to work for you on LinkedIn by claiming IU as your alma mater on your profile.

It’s also a good idea to follow your campus page to search for fellow IU alumni. You’ll receive updates, career advice and articles, and links to IU career events.

Find a little inspiration

Whether we need a little boost to get to the next level, or feel like we are running out of juice, we can recharge our professional batteries with helpful career wisdom and inspiring stories of career success. 

Best of all, these helpful, career-boosting resources are free to all IU alumni.

  • Career Minded podcasts—IU alums give candid interviews about their careers and offer advice
  • Pro-Tip videos—IU alums give their very best career advice on a variety of topics
  • Career Navigator webcast series—Get professional advice from career experts

Or learn new skills

To get where we want to go, it’s often necessary to learn new skills. IU offers a number of resources that can help us do it.

Lifelong Learning

IU Online

Take a class or get another degree from wherever you are!

IT Training

Pick up new IT skills or add a certification to your resume.
