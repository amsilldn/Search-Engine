Alumni & Giving

Join a worldwide network of 690,000+ Indiana University graduates

IU alumni are impressive for many reasons: their sheer numbers, how you can run into them practically anywhere, their incredible success. The most remarkable thing about IU grads? The way they support IU, students, and each other throughout their lives.

Your IU network includes . . .

  • Sage Steele, ESPN anchor

  • Deepender Singh Hooda, a member of India’s parliament
  • Joshua Bell, virtuoso violinist

Enhance your career through the IU Alumni Association

Whatever stage you’re at in your professional life, the IU Alumni Association (IUAA) will help you manage your career.

Network with IU alumni and tap into their career wisdom. Seek advice from career coaches. Attend professional development events, in person or online. And that’s just the start of how the IUAA helps students and alumni.
