Success Stories

IU students prepare for a lifetime of success

Every IU student has a different definition of success. Some want the corner office and the title to go with it. Some want a well-traveled passport with the stamps to prove it. Some just want to leave the world a better place, even if they don’t get any credit for making it happen.

Whatever their ultimate goals are, whatever journey they take to get there, they take their first steps toward fulfilling their promise while they’re at IU.
