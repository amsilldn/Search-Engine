Admissions

Find what you’re looking for at Indiana University

As an international student, you want a university with highly ranked academic programs, a first-class reputation, and an environment where you can feel at home. At Indiana University, you’ll find all of this—and more.

Learn more about IU Bloomington in our viewbook

Take a virtual tour

Tour the IU Bloomington campus from anywhere in the world.

Start the tour

Join the mailing list

Join our mailing list to learn more about IU Bloomington. We'll send periodic emails with student stories, information about what life is like at IU, and reminders about important application deadlines.

Join the mailing list

Find out how to become a student at IU

Whether you want to apply as a bachelor’s degree–seeking student (“freshman”), transfer from another college or university, or attend graduate school, we will guide you through the application process.

Learn how to apply

Explore options for financing your education

The Office of International Services (OIS) can help you determine the cost of your education, and connect you with resources available to pay for it—including scholarships, loans, and non-IU sources of funding.

Learn more about tuition and financial aid

We are here to help you

Contact OIS

You can always contact us with any questions about IU Bloomington.

Contact an International Student Ambassador

Our International Student Ambassadors can give you the perspective of an international student at IU Bloomington.
